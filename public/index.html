The form captures contact, address, work scope, service type, timeframe, photos, and description.

2. AI quote draft

The AI builds missing questions, labor phases, material lists, live pricing, risks, and quote totals.

3. Admin review

Nothing is auto-sent. Admin reviews and edits the draft before sending a final quote.
