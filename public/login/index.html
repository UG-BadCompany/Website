Client Portal Access

Open your Client Portal with a secure magic link.

Enter the email you used when requesting work from T&A Contracting. We will send a one-time secure link to open your client dashboard.

If you do not receive an email within a few minutes, check spam or contact T&A Contracting so we can confirm the email on your account.

  • View job requests, estimated start dates, completion updates, and quotes connected to your account.
  • Submit another request for a saved property or add a new property from your dashboard.
  • No password is required; each sign-in uses a fresh secure email link.

Send magic link

Enter your email to request a secure Client Portal link. For your privacy, links expire automatically and can only be used once.

How account access works

  • 1. Request an estimate or contact T&A Contracting so we can connect your email to your client account.
  • 2. Use this page to request a secure one-time sign-in link.
  • 3. Open the link from your email to view requests, saved properties, quotes, and schedule updates.
  • 4. Need help? Contact T&A Contracting and we can update your account details.
