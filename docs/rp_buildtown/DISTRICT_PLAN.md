# RP_BUILDTOWN District Plan

This blockout follows the attached RP_BUILDTOWN island concept: central civic spawn, downtown to the west, apartments and hospital to the north, park to the east, beach to the south, farmland northwest, industrial southwest, build zones east/southeast, and mountain forest northeast.

## Districts

| # | District | VMF blockout location | Gameplay purpose | Key inclusions |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Spawn / Plaza | Center | Orientation, safe arrival, city hall frontage | Player spawns, plaza pad, city hall, bunker hatch |
| 2 | Shops / Downtown | West-center | Dense RP storefronts and street roleplay | Bank, police department, vehicle dealership |
| 3 | Apartments | North-center | Residential RP | Two apartment tower blockouts |
| 4 | Hospital | North / northeast of apartments | Emergency and medical RP | Hospital main building pad |
| 5 | Park / Recreation | East of spawn | Social area and light recreation | Green pad, open paths, room for gazebo/playground polish |
| 6 | Beach / Waterfront | South-center coast | Waterfront RP | Sand district, lifeguard blockout, dock polish area |
| 7 | Farmland | Northwest | Rural jobs and farming RP | Farm barn and open field pads |
| 8 | Build Zone A | East | Large BuildRP plots | Six marked plots |
| 9 | Build Zone B | Southeast | Secondary BuildRP plots | Six marked plots |
| 10 | Industrial / Construction | Southwest | Construction, factory, logistics RP | Factory, construction company yard office |
| 11 | Mountain / Forest | Northeast | Natural exploration and tunnels | Cliff mass, forest area, tunnel portal indicators |

## Required special locations

- **Bank:** downtown west-center, near the main road grid.
- **Police department:** downtown, close to spawn for quick response.
- **Vehicle dealership:** downtown edge with road access.
- **Construction company yard:** industrial district, adjacent to factory space.
- **Underground admin bunker:** under spawn plaza with a hatch marker for a future detailed entrance.
- **Mountain tunnel system:** northeast mountain blockout with portal/corridor markers for future displacement or carved geometry.
- **Road network:** north-south and east-west arterial roads connect all districts with loops through farmland, industrial, build zones, and mountain forest.

## Blockout conventions

- District pads use dev, concrete, grass, sand, brick, or rock materials so areas are visually separable in Hammer.
- Named VMF editor comments identify each major solid section.
- `info_target` entities mark district centers for navigation, scripting, and future reference.
- `info_player_start` entities are clustered around Spawn / Plaza for immediate playtesting.
